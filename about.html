About Us

Located far away in Pankshin on the Plateau within the Middle Belt, The Farm House acts as a middle man between farmers in rural areas and cunsumers in urbarn areas. The major purpose of the farm house is to:

  • Limit food wastage when farmers harvest their crops and there are no people to buy.
  • Reduce poverty among farmers since they beat down their prices so as to sell out their produce while fresh.
  • Curb food scarcity in urban areas due to the inability to get farm produce.
As a result of this, farming continues round the yea as farmers are not afraid that what they harvest will spoil since The Farm house keeps proper storage of all farm produce. Those in urban areas have constant supply of farm produce as The Farm House gives constant supply to markets, individuals and superstores.
